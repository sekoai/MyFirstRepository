About This Blog

This blog documents the real-life success stories of people from Serutle and Manamela villages who started with very little but through hard work, determination and smart decisions built wealth and improved their lives.

Many began as teenagers working in village shops, for local farmers, or doing odd jobs - often being paid in livestock like cows, goats or sheep as part of annual contracts.

Today, these individuals own assets like cars, trucks, businesses, and some have relocated to Maseru while maintaining connections to their home villages.

Their stories are meant to inspire and educate the next generation about the possibilities that exist even when starting from humble beginnings.
