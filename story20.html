Lineo’s Journey: From Manamela to Global Online Income

Lineo from Manamela village always had big dreams, but limited opportunities. After finishing high school with no funds to go to university, she stayed home, frustrated — until she watched a YouTube video on “how to earn money online”.

She taught herself graphic design, freelancing, and digital marketing using free content from platforms like YouTube and Coursera. With a cheap smartphone and borrowed Wi-Fi, she joined freelance platforms like Fiverr and Upwork.

Slowly, Lineo built a strong profile. She began designing logos, business cards, and flyers for clients across Africa. She later opened her own website and digital coaching program to help other girls in villages learn freelancing.

Today, Lineo earns more than most office jobs pay — all from her phone and laptop. She now teaches online skills to young women in Serutle and Manamela, helping them avoid idleness and poverty.

Her story proves that even without a degree, online learning and determination can open doors to global success.
