Nthabiseng’s Journey: From Preschool Teacher to School Owner and Author

After completing her college studies in education, Nthabiseng began her career teaching at a humble preschool in Manamela. With passion for children and a vision for quality learning, she gave her all in the classroom, planting seeds of growth in her young learners.

Years later, Nthabiseng decided to expand her dream and relocated to Thaba-Tseka, where she started a small learning center with just a few children. Driven by excellence and innovation, her school grew rapidly as parents saw results. Her love for education turned into a powerful mission.

Today, she owns a successful private school in Thaba-Tseka with over 400 students enrolled. She didn’t stop there — Nthabiseng authored and published several books used by learners and teachers across the region.

Her story is one of dedication, vision, and service to the community. Nthabiseng has become a wealthy and respected woman, using her success to uplift others and continue shaping the future through education.
